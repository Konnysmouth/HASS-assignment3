HDB Resale Price Data Visualization

./Data from Data.gov.sg
■

./BACKGROUND: In order to get a general understanding of the development trend of the HDB resale price, a data visualization is created using D3 libraries and data source from Data.gov.sg.


■

./CONCEPT: This data visualization shows the relationship between the "Lease Commence Year" and the "HDB Resale Price". From here, viewers could get the overall trend of the increasement of the HDB resale price regarding to their lease commence date. From the visulization, we could see that the overall trend is increasing but the resale price for those that commenced on lease remained flat after 1992.
